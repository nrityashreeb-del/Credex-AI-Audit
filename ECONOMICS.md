# Unit Economics & Business Value

## The "Hook" Strategy
The AI Spend Auditor serves as a high-intent lead generation tool for Credex. By providing immediate financial value (showing savings), it builds trust with the user before a sales conversation begins.

## Conversion Metrics
- **Threshold for High Value:** If the tool identifies >$500 in annual savings, the user is prompted with a priority "Book a Consultation" CTA.
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Because this is a self-service tool, the CAC is significantly lower than traditional cold-outreach methods.

## Value Proposition
1. **Cost Avoidance:** Identifies users paying for duplicate "Pro" licenses.
2. **Security Premium:** While the tool identifies cost savings, it sets the stage for Credex to upsell on security. Companies are often willing to pay a 20% premium (moving from $20 individual accounts to $25/30 team accounts) to gain administrative control and data privacy.

## Market Data
Research suggests "Shadow AI" (unauthorized personal subscriptions used for work) accounts for significant untracked spending. This tool captures that data and brings it into the official procurement conversation.
