# User Interview Records

## Interview 1: The Hidden Spender
- **Profile:** Junior Analyst at a tech firm.
- **Insight:** Pays for a personal ChatGPT Plus account to stay productive but doesn't expense it to avoid "IT bureaucracy."
- **Takeaway:** Companies undercount their actual AI spend because it's hidden in personal employee expenses.

## Interview 2: The Concerned Manager
- **Profile:** IT Lead at a 20-person agency.
- **Insight:** Concerned about data privacy on free/individual accounts. Would pay a 20% premium for a "Team" plan just for the security console.
- **Takeaway:** The value proposition isn't just "Saving Money," it's "Buying Security."

## Interview 3: The Startup Founder
- **Profile:** CEO of a Seed-stage startup.
- **Insight:** Overwhelmed by different tool prices (OpenAI vs. Anthropic). Wants a single "Source of Truth" for AI spending.
- **Takeaway:** There is high demand for a tool that simplifies comparison across different AI vendors.
