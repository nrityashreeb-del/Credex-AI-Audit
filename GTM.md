# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Founders and Ops Managers of startups (10-50 employees) where "SaaS sprawl" is most common.
- **Secondary:** IT procurement officers in mid-market firms looking to consolidate "Shadow AI" accounts.

## Distribution Channels
1. **LinkedIn Lead Magnet:** Sharing the Auditor tool link in "Operations" and "SaaS Management" groups.
2. **Cold Outreach:** Using the tool as a "free gift" in email outreach to CEOs—offering a personalized audit report in exchange for a 15-minute call.
3. **Product-Led Growth:** Allowing users to download their "Savings Report" as a PDF, which includes a Credex referral link.
