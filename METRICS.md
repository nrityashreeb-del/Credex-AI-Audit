# Success Metrics (KPIs)

## Product Metrics
- **Audit Completion Rate:** The percentage of users who land on the page and complete the 2-step form.
- **Average Identified Savings:** The total dollar amount of waste found per user (indicates tool accuracy).

## Business Metrics
- **Lead Conversion Rate:** The percentage of users who click "Book Consultation" after seeing their savings report.
- **Virality Coefficient:** Tracking how many users share their unique report URL with team members.
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** Goal is to keep CAC <$50 by using this tool as an organic lead generator.
